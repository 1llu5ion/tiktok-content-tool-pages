Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

1. Overview

TikTok Content Tool is used to upload approved short videos to TikTok through official TikTok APIs after the TikTok account owner authorizes access.

2. Information we collect

Depending on the permissions granted by the user, the tool may receive limited TikTok account information such as a user identifier and basic profile information, OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens, and video upload or publishing status returned by TikTok. The tool may also process video files, captions, and related metadata provided by the user for upload.

3. How information is used

Information is used only to authenticate the user, upload approved video content to TikTok as drafts or approved posts, check upload status, troubleshoot errors, and maintain the content workflow requested by the user.

4. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information is shared with TikTok only as necessary to use TikTok's official APIs and with service providers only as necessary to operate the tool.

5. Data retention

OAuth tokens and upload metadata are retained only as long as needed for the content workflow or troubleshooting. Users may request deletion of stored data or revoke TikTok access from their TikTok account settings.

6. Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to protect tokens and content files. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so users should avoid uploading sensitive or unauthorized content.

7. User choices

Users can stop using the tool at any time, request deletion of stored workflow data, and revoke access through TikTok's connected apps or account settings.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the repository owner through GitHub: https://github.com/1llu5ion.
